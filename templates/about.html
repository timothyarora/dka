03 : About me

About Me

Hi, I’m Dr. Deepak Kumar, an experienced and research-driven Electrical Engineer with a strong specialization in Power Electronics and Embedded Systems. I hold a Ph.D. in Power Electronics from the National Institute of Technology, India, where I focused on the design, analysis, and control of a three-port battery-integrated dc-dc converters for LVDC systems.

I also earned my M.Tech in Power Electronics and Drives from the National Institute of Technology, India, and my B.E. in Electrical Engineering from C.R. State College of Engineering, Murthal (now DCRUST), building a solid foundation in both theoretical and applied electrical engineering concepts.

With over a decade of experience across academia and industry, I’ve held roles as an Assistant Professor and currently work as an Embedded Systems Engineer at Westhill Innovation Inc. (Canada). My projects have ranged from embedded communication systems and PCB design to high-efficiency power converters and system-level simulations.

Core Technical Skills

  • Programming: C, C++, MATLAB, Python
  • Embedded Systems: Arduino, 8051, STM32
  • PCB Design: Eagle, Altium, KiCad, Proteus
  • Electrical Simulations: PSIM, MULTISIM, LTSPICE, MATLAB/Simulink
  • AutoCAD Electrical Design
  • Technical & Academic Writing: Journal articles, conference papers, book chapters, technical reports

I’ve published multiple peer-reviewed papers, contributed to academic books, and supported clients worldwide with freelance projects involving electrical system design, simulations, and embedded development. Whether it’s academic consultation, R&D, or prototyping, I bring clarity, precision, and a solution-oriented mindset to every project.
